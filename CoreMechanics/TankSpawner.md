# Tank spawner

The tank spawners are what you use to spawn your tanks each team gets at least one and you can heve one tank spawned in at a time.
Upon interacting with it you will open a menu in wich you can see all the tanks you unlocked.

## How do i unlock a tank

It's easy just go back to the main menu by resseting and open the tank UI. Here you can buy the tanks you want.
